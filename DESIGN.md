# Design

## Direction
整体院の新規集客LP。白基調の清潔な面、深いネイビーの専門性、控えめなゴールドの価格帯感を組み合わせる。添付写真を主役にし、装飾は薄い線、余白、ガラス調の予約導線に限定する。

## Palette
Base: oklch(99% 0.003 250)
Ink: oklch(20% 0.05 255)
Navy: oklch(25% 0.08 255)
Navy Deep: oklch(18% 0.075 255)
Gold: oklch(70% 0.13 85)
Sage: oklch(55% 0.08 145)
Mist: oklch(95% 0.01 250)

## Typography
Shippori Mincho B1 is used for Japanese body and headings to preserve trust and calm authority.
Pinyon Script is used sparingly for small English accents.
Fascinate Inline is used only for the brand monogram so it does not compete with Japanese text.

## Components
Sticky header with primary reservation CTA.
Hero with image-led proof blocks.
Pain cards with line icons.
Comparison diagram for temporary relief versus root-cause care.
Reason cards with trust markers.
Flow timeline.
Testimonials and before-after examples marked as replaceable examples.
FAQ accordion.
Sticky mobile reservation bar.

## Motion
Subtle fade and slide reveals using IntersectionObserver.
Hover lift on CTA and cards.
No motion-critical content; all content is visible without JavaScript.
